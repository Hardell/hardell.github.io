Emerging from the vibrant music scene of Prague, Czech Republic, IONS is a dynamic five-piece progressive metal outfit. Founded by guitarist Jakub Tirčo and vocalist Tomas "Shorty" Lago, the band's inception was a natural progression of their initial collaboration on Tirčo's EP, "Introspection".
IONS' distinctive sound is characterized by a seamless integration of atmospheric keyboards, orchestral elements, and complex time signatures. Their affinity for synthesizers, low-tuned guitars, and post-rock influences shines in their latest album "Counterintuitive".
Within their songwriting, the band explores themes of morality, inequality in society, animal rights, domestic abuse, and more.
