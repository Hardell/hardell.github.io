IONS is a five-piece progressive metal outfit based in Prague, Czech Republic.
IONS originally started as a solo project of the band's guitarist Jakub Tirco. Back in 2016, he
joined forces with the lead singer Shorty Lago for the first time on his solo release Jakub Tirco –
Introspection. Although his initial plan for it was to be a one-time collaboration, something
brought them back together, he says. “A few years passed, and suddenly in 2019, we were
releasing a full-length album together. It was pretty obvious that it was no longer a solo project,
but soon-to-become a band.”
The project fully transitioned to being a live band in 2022 with their new single “A Terrible
Mistake” followed by a series of successful club shows in the Czech Republic and Slovakia.
